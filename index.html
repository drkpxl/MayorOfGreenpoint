About Mike

Mike has been a fixture in the Greenpoint community for over 42 years. Known by just about everyone in the neighborhood, he's earned his title as the "Mayor of Greenpoint" through his friendly nature, ownership of the corner of the Moonlight Mile, the copious amount of dog pee on all sidewalks, and his willingness to help anyone in need who isn't a oilers fan.


His helpfulness and love of 69 Chevelles can only be beat by his love of late model Toyota Camrys.


Whether you need restaurant recommendations, where to get a bottle of High Life, or just want to know the history behind that quirky building on the corner, Mike is your go-to person. His passion for Greenpoint is matched only by his genuine love for the people who call it home. Unless of course you are a a bag of dicks that doesn't know how to drive
